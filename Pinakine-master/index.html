Pinakine Cottages is situated in a very beautiful place known as Nehru Kund which is located at the distance of around 4 kms from Manali.One can also travel to old Manali from the location of our cottage which is of very less distance . Just when you drive through the bridge which is the entry for the Nehru kind place all you have to travel for this beautiful cottage is very less . Nehru kund is one of the natural springs which has natural flowing water in India. Nehru kind is named after the Late Prime Minister Pundit Jawar Lal Nehru .While Late Shi Jawar Lal Nehru used to travel to Manali, he used to drink water from the Nehru Kund and this kund is situated on the Manali-Keylong road. The graceful clean water spring is said to have its source at the Bhrigu Lake. The sound of the bubbling water amidst the peaceful silence of the place will play like music to your ears. The river Beas flows through it. The serenity of the place and crystal clear water of the Kund attracts the tourists who visit Manali. Nehru Kund is an integral part of the Manali sight seeing tours and when travelling through Manali , one shouldn't miss visiting this place.
